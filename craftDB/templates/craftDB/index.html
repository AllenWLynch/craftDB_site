CraftDB Endpoints

craftDB/items ? modpack = modpack_name

Returns new-line delineated list of unique item names saved in the specified modpack.


craftDB/instructions ? for = unique_item_name & quantity = number_to_make & inventory = inventory_dict (json)

Returns a lua-serialized queue for crafting with recipeID and quantity of each step.


craftDB/recipes/id

Returns in-depth information for crafting of specified recipe.
